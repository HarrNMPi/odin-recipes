Lasagne

Description

Lasagne are a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets.

Can can also refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagne alternating with fillings

Ingredients
  • 1 Store bought lasagne
  • Cutlery
  • Red Wine
  1. Preheat oven
  2. Pierce film and place on tray
  3. Put in oven for 40 minutes
  4. Allow to cool and then eat
